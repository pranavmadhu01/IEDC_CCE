SKONIX

A Robotics education initiative


SKONIX


Mohammed Ashik S, Krishnaprasad C,  Anson Jose and Albin Joseph C R set out with an initiative to conduct Robotics and Arduino based workshops in some schools and colleges throughout Thrissur. They sought to bridge the gap between academics and the realtime industry. That initiative is what we now know as Skonix.
