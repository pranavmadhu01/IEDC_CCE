3D Printing Workshop

Date : 23/11/2021
The Department of Civil Engineering in association with IEDC-CCE conducted a hands-on 3D Printing Workshop on the 23rd of November 2021. The workshop was exclusively organized for the 5th semester civil engineering students, and the resource persons from Astrek Innovations included Mr. Robin Kanattu Thomas (Co-founder), Mr. Sidharth Sivadasan (Product Designer), and Mr. Vishnu Sankar (Head of Partnerships).
The workshop commenced formally at 9:30 am, and the whole class was divided into six groups of ten members each for ease of functioning. The class was initially shown some previously printed models, in order to touch and feel the texture and detailing. The simulations of the models were then designed on the web in a platform called Tinkercad, in which the tools were familiarized to each and every student attending the session. The students were able to sketch, project and create in their respective laptops on their own, owing to the proficiency of resource persons involved. The workshop explained the particulars of 3D printing and the internal processes involved during the production of an object. Topics spanning from the materials used, time required and the durability of products were discussed in detail apart from the hands-on experience. At the end of the day, each group had to present their own model from a marketing point of view, which helped deepen the perception regarding utility of models. Afterwards, the best model was printed in front of the whole class. Each stage of printing was demonstrated painstakingly to attain enhanced comprehension.
The session was winded up by 4:30 pm on a grateful note by Dr. Krishnapriya M. G., head of the Department of Civil Engineering, Christ College of Engineering. The day surely proved to be immensely productive for the entire class and thereby received excellent feedback for the level of understanding and perception gained during the entirety of the workshop.

Regional Level IDEATHON

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre of Christ College of Engineering, Irinjalakuda hosted the regional level IDEATHON as part of Innovators' Premier League on 4th, 5th of January 2022.
The event was conducted in online mode via Google Meet. The two days of IDEATHON was efficiently organized by giving different time slots and links to participants. The students were informed about their respective time slots and were asked to present their ideas within the scheduled time using their various tools for presentation. The morning session began at 9:30 am and extended till 1pm. The afternoon session began at 1:30 pm and concluded by 4pm.
The esteemed judging panel included Dr.Ansal (Asst.Professor(Adhoc ) NIT, Goa ), Mr.Jithin (COO,Astrek Innovation, Kochi ), Mr. Sharon (Regional Lead, Nascomm, Ernakulam). They had spend their valuable time for the young aspiring minds with creative ideas. From 46 colleges, 52 teams participated in the event. The students effectively used the golden platform to think out of box and express their vibrant ideas.
Over 50+ students took part in the workshop with great zeal. It provided a deeper insight into the innovative world of 3D printing from its basic software to it's real life application enabling participants to breach the gap between imagination and technology.Each team used power point presentations for expressing their innovative. Total 10 minutes was alloted for each team and it was further divided into 5 - 5 minutes. First 5 minutes for presentation and rest 5 minutes for the judges to interact with the participants. The judges keenly listened to their ideas and gave them valuable suggestions for making it into a prototype. The interactive session helped the students to know their weak points and to further develop their ideas.

Aspiring Entrepreneurs Program 2021

The Aspiring Entrepreneurs program 2021 was launched by the ICT Academy in association with IEDC CCE for the youth in Kerala and Tamil Nadu is a CSR initiative of HDFC Bank Parivartan, implemented by ICT Academy. The program equipped young aspiring entrepreneurs and students with competence, confidence and connections to build a strong foundation for their entrepreneurial journey.
This program consisted of 3 stages namely- Entrepreneurship awareness session, Entrepreneurship training program and Mentoring and facilitation. It also provided course handouts, access to self-learning, training by experts, successful entrepreneur’s sessions, certificates for every stage and personalized mentoring. Aspiring students in the age group of 20-25, who are keen on pursuing entrepreneurship, were eligible to apply for this program.
Around 80+ students participated for the sessions that was truly valuable.The event was a great success and students were able to get a basic outline of the various stages of entrepreneurship and its development.Thus it was another wonderful opportunity young aspiring minds.
